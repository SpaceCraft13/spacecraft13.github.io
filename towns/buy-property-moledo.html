Frequently Asked Questions

Can foreigners buy property in Portugal?

Yes. International buyers can buy property in Portugal. Most buyers first obtain a Portuguese tax number, called a NIF, and open a local bank account before completing a purchase.

Is Northern Portugal better value than Lisbon or the Algarve?

For many buyers, yes. Northern Portugal often offers more space, lower entry prices, and a calmer lifestyle while still providing beauty, culture, and access to international travel.
