Guide Summary

Move & Invest in Northern Portugal is positioned as a relocation and investment guide powered by Spacious. It explains why the north offers a slower pace of life, authentic culture, safety, affordability, and strong lifestyle value for international buyers.

Caminha Viana do Castelo Ponte de Lima Minho Region Relocation + Investment

Why Northern Portugal

The guide highlights Atlantic beaches, mountain trails, the Minho River, historic towns, strong food culture, and a lower cost of living than much of Western Europe. It frames the region as a compelling alternative to the more over-touristed south of Portugal.

Visa pathways covered

D7 Visa

For applicants with passive income, plus banking, accommodation, health insurance, and a clean record.

Digital Nomad Visa

For remote workers or business owners meeting income thresholds and documentation requirements.

Entrepreneur Visa

For those building a viable business in Portugal with investment and job creation potential.

Golden Visa

Described as an investment-based path with low stay requirements and possible long-term residency benefits.

NIF, banking, and buying property

The guide explains that a Portuguese tax number is essential for major transactions including banking, property purchases, leases, business formation, and visa processes. It also outlines the property buying flow: finding the right property, due diligence, promissory contract, final deed, registration, taxes, and financing.

Income strategies and wealth positioning

It presents several property-use strategies, including short-term rentals, long-term rentals, boutique hospitality, and hybrid owner-use models. The guide also frames Northern Portugal real estate as a hedge against inflation, volatility, and geopolitical uncertainty.

How Spacious helps

The final section positions Spacious as a local partner for property discovery, renovation and design, property management, and relocation support from the first search through long-term ownership.
